WHY DOES THIS EXIST?


I recently learned that bargaining power significantly impacts a person’s earnings... but I suck at bargaining. I don't know how my labour can be valued, and how it might compare to other industries.

So I made this website to familiarize myself with how labour can be valued.

There's a short quiz that randomly populates with government census data to challenge your knowledge. There's also interactive charts so you can navigate the data after you play.

I had basically zero web-dev knowledge when I started making this in 5 days so it isn't perfect, but it's a start. I hope you enjoy!
