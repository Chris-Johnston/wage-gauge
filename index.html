WHY DOES THIS EXIST?


I recently learned that bargaining power significantly impacts a person’s earnings... but I suck at bargaining.

After thinking about why, I realized it’s because Iassume the other person has more information and they’re making a fair assessment of my value.

That is rarely that case when it comes to wages.

I made this website because I think it’s important for us to know how our labour is being valued - even if it’s an estimate.

The quiz utilizes government survey data between 2017 - 2021. You can read more about it in the methodology linked in the footer.
